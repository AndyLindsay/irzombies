IR ZOMBIES!!!

Fun humans vs zombies game for the Open Source Hardware Summit 2015 badge!

Playing

You'll need at least 3 players, 2 of which should choose to be a Survivor (S). Each player may use the OSH button below the OLED to attack, heal, or infect others over IR. It's the standard zombie apocalypse paradigm: zombies infect survivors, survivors attack zombies and heal infected, infected turn into zombies if not healed.
